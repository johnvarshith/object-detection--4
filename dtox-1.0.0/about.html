YOLO-You Only Look Once

YOLOv1 (2016) was the first version of YOLO, introducing the concept of real-time object detection. YOLOv2 (2017) improved upon YOLOv1 with faster speed and better accuracy. YOLOv3 (2018) further enhanced the performance of YOLO, introducing a new network architecture and improved object detection capabilities. YOLOv4 (2020) was a significant advancement, incorporating various techniques to achieve state-of-the-art performance. YOLOv5 (2020) was developed by Ultralytics and is known for its ease of use and high performance.

YOLOv6 (2021) was developed by Meituan and focuses on improving speed and accuracy. YOLOv7 (2022) was developed by Chien-Yao Wang and Alexey Bochkovskiy and is currently the most advanced version of YOLO, achieving impressive performance in terms of speed and accuracy. YOLOv8 (2023) was developed by Ultralytics and is the latest version of YOLO, offering even better performance and features.
